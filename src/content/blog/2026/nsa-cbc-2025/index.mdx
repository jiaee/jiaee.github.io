Hello! After not completing the 2024 NSA Codebreaker Challenge, I decided to actually finish the 2025 one, since I also didn't do FLARE-ON last year. This year, the challenge had 7 tasks, which I will be going over. Each task will be in its own subpost on the side, and this will just be a post with my overall thoughts and reflections on the challenge.

Personally I felt that this year had fairly good tasks, with the exception of task 2, which will be discussed more in its own post. It was a good mix of all the usual topics, with maybe a bit less focus on exploitation and more on forensics, but overall a good variety. I also felt like the difficulty was well balanced and ramped up well (at least after task 2). This is a little biased since I mainly do reverse engineering so the middle few tasks were easier for me.
